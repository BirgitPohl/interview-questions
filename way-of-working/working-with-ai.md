# Working with AI

## Describe a situation where your team colleagues used AI to code. What do you think will happen?
- As an interviewer you are asking about the existence of experience working with AI in a team or alone. It's a great entry question for more more follow up questions to ask.

## It turns out, that your colleagues are approving pull requests, but you noticed a lot of mistakes inside the pull requests. Since your colleagues use AI to code, what type of mistakes are typically visible using AI?
- AI often leads developers to be lazy and without awareness of the code, because AI is seemingly taking the job from us. But AI is yet not capable of doing it. So we need to mind the code quality.
- This questions also shows engineering quality in an interviewee. The person will be able to explain, based on experience, what AI is producing, vs what code design is desired.

## Your colleagues have difficulties understanding the code. They use AI to code. What would you recommend? And how would you like to help your colleagues adapt and change?
- The interviewee knows how to debug and code without AI
- and has possibilities to teach their co-workers how to debug and code without AI
